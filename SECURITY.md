# Security Policy

## Security Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

The security of our users and our systems is of the utmost importance. If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in our software or systems, we encourage you to report it to us.

### How to Report a Vulnerability

To report a vulnerability, please contact us at the following email address:

**Email:** security@resonanceOS.streamer

Please provide as much detail as possible when reporting the issue, including:
- A detailed description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the vulnerability
- Any relevant screenshots or logs

We will acknowledge your report within 72 hours and aim to provide a resolution or update within 30 days.

### Contact Instructions

If you prefer to contact us via alternative methods, you can also reach out through our official communication channels listed on our website.

Thank you for helping us keep our software and users secure!
